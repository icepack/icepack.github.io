icepack.meshing module¶

Utilities for turning glacier outlines into unstructured meshes

icepack.meshing.collection_to_geo(collection, lcar=10000.0)[source]¶

Convert a GeoJSON FeatureCollection into pygmsh geometry that can then be transformed into an unstructured triangular mesh

icepack.meshing.normalize(input_collection)[source]¶

Normalize a GeoJSON feature collection into a form that can easily be transformed into the input for a mesh generator
