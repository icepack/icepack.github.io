Reading
=======

Doug MacAyeal's `Lessons on Ice Sheet Modeling <http://geosci.uchicago.edu/pdfs/macayeal/lessons.pdf>`_ is a great reference with plenty of example code.
The standard textbook on glaciology is *The Physics of Glaicers* :cite:`cuffey2010physics`.
For more of a focus on modeling, see *Dynamics of Ice Sheets and Glaciers* :cite:`greve2009dynamics`.
*Understanding and Implementing the Finite Element Method* is a good introduction :cite:`gockenbach2006understanding` while *Finite Elements: Theory, Fast Solvers, and Applications in Solid Mechanics* :cite:`braess2007finite` is a more advanced treatment on numerically solving partial differential equations.
