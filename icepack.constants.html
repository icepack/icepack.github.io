icepack.constants module¶

Physical constants

This module constains physical constants used throughout the library such as the acceleration due to gravity, the universal gas constant, the density of ice and water, etc. Like Elmer/Ice, we use units of megapascals, meters, and years throughout. This choice of units makes the rate factor (see icepack.models.viscosity.rate_factor()) roughly equal to 1 for typical temperatures.

icepack.constants.glen_flow_law = 3.0¶

exponent in the nonlinear constitutive law for ice

icepack.constants.gravity = 9769603575225600.0¶

acceleration due to gravity (m / yr^2)

icepack.constants.heat_capacity = 1.99176423552e+18¶

specific heat capacity of ice at -10C (m^2 / yr^2 / K)

icepack.constants.ice_density = 9.207917118369125e-19¶

density of ice

icepack.constants.ideal_gas = 0.0083144621¶

ideal gas constant (kJ / mol K)

icepack.constants.latent_heat = 3.3262462733184e+20¶

latent heat of melting of ice (m^2 / yr^2)

icepack.constants.melting_temperature = 273.15¶

melting point of ice at atmospheric pressure (K)

icepack.constants.thermal_diffusivity = 39.57605234460196¶

thermal diffusivity of ice at -10C (m^2 / yr)

icepack.constants.water_density = 1.0282341471330407e-18¶
