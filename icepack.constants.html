icepack.constants module¶

Physical constants

This module constains physical constants used throughout the library such as the acceleration due to gravity, the universal gas constant, the density of ice and water, etc. Like Elmer/Ice, we use units of megapascals, meters, and years throughout. This choice of units makes the rate factor (see icepack.viscosity.rate_factor()) roughly equal to 1 for typical temperatures.
