icepack.norms module¶

icepack.norms.norm(u, norm_type='L2')[source]¶

Compute the norm of a field

Computes one of any number of norms of a scalar or vector field. The available options are:

  • L2: \(\|u\|^2 = \int_\Omega|u|^2dx\)

  • H01: \(\|u\|^2 = \int_\Omega|\nabla u|^2dx\)

  • H1: \(\|u\|^2 = \int_\Omega\left(|u|^2 + L^2|\nabla u|^2\right)dx\)

  • L1: \(\|u\| = \int_\Omega|u|dx\)

  • TV: \(\|u\| = \int_\Omega|\nabla u|dx\)

  • Linfty: \(\|u\| = \max_{x\in\Omega}|u(x)|\)

The extra factor \(L\) in the \(H^1\) norm is the diameter of the domain in the infinity metric. This extra factor is included to make the norm scale appropriately with the size of the domain.
